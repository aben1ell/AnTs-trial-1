Our Story

Craftsmanship,
excellence &
shared indulgence

KOBA is Addis Ababa's home for handcrafted pastries, cakes, and breads made with the finest local and globally-sourced ingredients. We believe the finest baking happens at the intersection of technique and genuine care.

Our pastry chefs never stop experimenting — testing new flavour combinations, refining textures, and pushing presentation to its most beautiful form.

Whether it's a quiet morning coffee with a croissant or an afternoon spent sharing a celebration cake, every moment at KOBA is worth remembering.
